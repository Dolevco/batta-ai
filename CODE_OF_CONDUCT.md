# Code of Conduct

We use the Contributor Covenant as the baseline for project behavior.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- using welcoming and inclusive language;
- respecting differing viewpoints and experience levels;
- giving constructive feedback;
- focusing on what is best for the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal
attacks, and publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions
that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in a temporary
or permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns to `security@example.com`.
